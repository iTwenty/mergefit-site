Privacy Policy - MergeFit

MergeFit is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users.

Usage Data

We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymized usage data. This helps us understand how our users are using our app and how we can improve it.

  • TelemetryDeck does not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • All data sent is fully anonymized and cannot be traced back to any individual.

You can read more in TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

Health Data

MergeFit uses Apple Health to read and write workout data, such as distance, duration and type of activity.

  • All health-related data accessed or generated by MergeFit stays entirely on your device.
  • This data is never uploaded to external servers or shared with third parties.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us.
