MergeFit

Easiest way to merge your Apple Health Workouts

Select

Easily select the workouts you want to merge from a list of all your workouts. Once you select a workout, other unsupported workouts are disabled.

Preview

Preview before merging to know exactly what the merged workout will look like. Preview shows important metrics like total distance, heart rate, energy burned etc. as well as the route for the merged workout.

Merge

Preview looks good? Simply tap the button and let MergeFit work its magic! MergeFit will read all relevant data from the selected workouts and create a new workout by combining all of it into a new workout.
