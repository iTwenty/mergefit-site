Merge effortlessly.

Merge multiple workouts of the same type into one complete workout. Preview key metrics like distance, heart rate, energy burned and full route before merging, so you know exactly what the result will be.

Like the preview? Tap Merge and let MergeFit put it all together into a single workout.

Trim the excess.

Trim any workout to cut out unwanted time from the start or end. Preview key metrics like distance, duration and route, so you know exactly what's getting trimmed.

Once it looks right, tap Trim and let MergeFit remove the excess.

Resolve overlaps.

When workouts overlap in time, the built-in timeline graph visually displays gaps and overlaps. Granular stream selection gives you complete control. Choose exactly which metrics (like Heart Rate, GPS route etc.) to keep from each workout.

Handle complex scenarios like merging cadence from your indoor bike trainer with HR from your watch.

Get notified.

MergeFit can automatically notify you whenever new workouts can be merged with existing ones.

Upload to Strava.

MergeFit integrates with Strava. Easily send your workouts to Strava from within the app.

Export as FIT file.

Need to use your data elsewhere? MergeFit can also export your workouts as universal FIT files.

Common Use Cases

Accidentally hit Stop instead of Pause? Start a new workout and merge the two sessions back into one continuous workout.

Forgot to hit Stop after finishing? Use trimming to keep only the meaningful part of your workout and drop the car ride home.

Recorded across devices to capture different metrics? Use stream selection to combine all streams into a single workout.
